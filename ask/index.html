What Lane helps with
  • Flood zones & insurance — what a zone means for a home, what changed after the 2024 storms, how coverage is priced.
  • Neighborhoods — the features, the flood story, and the housing stock of the areas we cover, chapter by chapter.
  • Property taxes — homestead, Save Our Homes, portability, and how the numbers actually work here.
  • Short-term rentals — where in Pinellas an Airbnb is actually permitted, municipality by municipality, with dates.
  • The process — buying steps and costs, and how a broker-prepared valuation differs from an instant online number.
How Lane stays honest
  • Grounded, not guessing. Lane draws on Everlane’s published, sourced knowledge base — every fact dated — and is built to say “I don’t know” and hand you to Serge rather than fill a gap.
  • It describes the place, never who “belongs” there. A fair-housing line Lane is built to and checked against — automatically, on every answer.
  • Not a listing search. For homes on the market it sends you to the search page; live listing results arrive with the data feed. It never fabricates a listing.
  • Human-backed. It doesn’t collect your details here and it never closes a deal — anything transactional goes to a free consult with the broker.
